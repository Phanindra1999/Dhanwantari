BACHELOR'S OF COMMERCE

Accounting

Accounting is the process of recording, classifying, and summarizing financial transactions. It is a core subject of B.Com because it provides students with a comprehensive understanding of financial reporting and analysis. Students learn about the principles of financial accounting and managerial accounting, including the preparation and interpretation of financial statements, budgeting, and cost accounting. Through this subject, students develop analytical skills, attention to detail, and critical thinking abilities that are essential for a career in the accounting and finance fields.

Economics

Economics is the study of how individuals, firms, and governments make decisions about how to allocate resources. It is a core subject of B.Com because it provides students with a broad understanding of the economy as a whole. Students learn about microeconomics and macroeconomics, including topics such as demand and supply, market structures, national income, and economic growth. Through this subject, students develop analytical skills, problem-solving abilities, and an understanding of the key economic concepts that underlie business decision-making.

Finance

Finance is the study of how individuals, businesses, and governments manage money. It is a core subject of B.Com because it provides students with a strong foundation in financial management. Students learn about investment decisions, financial analysis, and risk management. Through this subject, students develop skills in financial modeling, data analysis, and financial planning that are essential for a career in finance.

Business Law

Business law is the study of legal issues that arise in the context of business activities. It is a core subject of B.Com because it provides students with an understanding of the legal framework within which businesses operate. Students learn about contracts, commercial law, company law, and intellectual property law. Through this subject, students develop skills in legal research, critical analysis, and problem-solving that are essential for a career in business law and related fields.

Taxation

Taxation is the study of how governments collect revenue from individuals and businesses. It is a core subject of B.Com because it provides students with an understanding of the principles of taxation. Students learn about income tax, sales tax, and value-added tax. Through this subject, students develop skills in tax planning, compliance, and research that are essential for a career in taxation and related fields.

Marketing

Marketing is the study of how businesses create, promote, and distribute products and services to customers. It is a core subject of B.Com because it provides students with an understanding of the principles of marketing. Students learn about market research, advertising, branding, and product development. Through this subject, students develop skills in communication, creativity, and strategic thinking that are essential for a career in marketing and related fields.
