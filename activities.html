ACTIVITIES

At our college, we understand that a well-rounded education involves more than just academics. That's why we offer a diverse range of student activities that allow students to explore their passions, connect with like-minded individuals, and develop new skills outside of the classroom.Whether you're interested in sports, the arts, community service, or politics, there's a student club or organization for you. Our student activities range from competitive athletic teams and performing arts groups to debate societies and volunteer organizations. These activities not only offer a fun and engaging way to spend your free time but also provide valuable opportunities for personal growth and leadership development.

Dhanwantari Academy for Management Studies, is a learning centre for undergraduate and post graduate programs.
