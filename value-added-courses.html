Value added Programs

At our college, we offer a range of value-added courses that provide students with the opportunity to enhance their skill set and gain practical experience in various fields. These courses are designed to complement and supplement the knowledge gained through traditional academic programs, providing students with a competitive edge in their future careers. The courses are taught by experienced professionals in their respective fields, who provide students with practical insights and hands-on training. Students who take these courses are better equipped to handle the challenges of the ever-evolving job market, and are more likely to secure top-notch jobs in their respective fields. These courses also provide students with networking opportunities and connections with industry professionals, opening doors to internships, jobs, and career advancement. At our college, we are committed to providing our students with a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in their chosen careers, and our value-added courses are a key part of that commitment.


Tally (ERP 9.0)

Our college offers a comprehensive Tally (ERP 9.0) course that provides students with the necessary skills to become proficient in one of the most widely used accounting software programs in the world. The course is designed to equip students with the technical knowledge required to handle various financial and accounting transactions, including inventory management, payroll management, and financial statements. Taught by experienced professionals in the field, the course offers hands-on training, enabling students to apply their learning to real-world scenarios. This course is particularly valuable for students interested in pursuing careers in finance, accounting, and business administration. Students who complete the Tally (ERP 9.0) course will have a significant advantage in the job market, as employers place a premium on proficiency in this software. Additionally, the course provides students with the opportunity to earn industry-recognized certifications, further enhancing their employability. At our college, we are committed to providing our students with the best possible education and training, and our Tally (ERP 9.0) course is just one of the many ways in which we do so.


Web Designing

Our college provides a comprehensive web designing course as a value-added course for students who wish to enhance their skill set and gain practical experience in the field of web development. This course is designed to teach students the fundamentals of web design, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and how to create visually appealing and functional websites. Taught by experienced professionals in the field, the course offers hands-on training, enabling students to create websites using the latest web design tools and techniques. This course is particularly valuable for students interested in pursuing careers in the tech industry, digital marketing, and e-commerce. Students who complete the web designing course will have a significant advantage in the job market, as employers place a premium on proficiency in web design. Additionally, the course provides students with the opportunity to work on real-world projects, building their portfolio and showcasing their skills to potential employers. At our college, we are committed to providing our students with a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in their chosen careers, and our web designing course is just one of the many ways in which we do so.


Advanced Excel

Our college offers a comprehensive Advanced Excel program as a value-added course for students who want to enhance their skills in data management and analysis. This course is designed to teach students how to use Excel's advanced features, including pivot tables, macros, and data visualization tools, to efficiently analyze and manipulate large datasets. Taught by experienced professionals in the field, the course offers hands-on training, enabling students to apply their learning to real-world scenarios. This course is particularly valuable for students interested in pursuing careers in finance, accounting, and data analysis. Students who complete the Advanced Excel program will have a significant advantage in the job market, as employers place a premium on proficiency in this software. Additionally, the course provides students with the opportunity to earn industry-recognized certifications, further enhancing their employability. At our college, we are committed to providing our students with the best possible education and training, and our Advanced Excel program is just one of the many ways in which we do so.


Coaching for Competitive Exams

Our college provides specialized coaching to help our students succeed in competitive examinations. Our experienced faculty members offer expert guidance and support to help students prepare for these exams, which are known for their rigor and complexity. We provide a comprehensive curriculum that covers all the necessary topics and concepts, along with extensive practice sessions, mock tests, and personalized feedback. Our coaching program has a proven track record of success, with many of our students achieving top ranks and securing admission in some of the country's most prestigious institutions. At our college, we are committed to helping our students realize their full potential, and our coaching program for competitive exams is just one of the many ways in which we do so.


Personality Development

Our college recognizes that success in today's world requires more than just academic excellence, and that is why we offer a comprehensive personality development course as a value-added program to our students. The course is designed to help students develop essential life skills, such as communication, interpersonal relations, time management, and leadership, that are crucial for success in any field. Our experienced and skilled faculty members provide personalized coaching and training, enabling students to identify their strengths and weaknesses and work on improving them. The course also provides opportunities for students to participate in group activities, debates, and discussions, which help them build confidence and hone their communication skills. Students who complete our personality development course will have a significant advantage in the job market, as employers value candidates with strong soft skills. Moreover, the course helps students become well-rounded individuals, equipped with the tools and knowledge to succeed in all aspects of life. At our college, we are committed to providing our students with a holistic education that prepares them for success in their chosen careers and personal lives, and our personality development course is just one of the many ways in which we do so.
