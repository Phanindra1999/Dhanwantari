Our Undergraduate Programs

Our college offers a wide range of undergraduate programs designed to provide students with a comprehensive education that prepares them for a successful career. Our programs are designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and critical thinking abilities that are essential in today's ever-changing job market. Our undergraduate programs provide a solid foundation in a variety of areas, allowing students to explore and discover their passions. Our faculty are dedicated to providing students with a supportive learning environment that encourages academic excellence and personal growth. Additionally, our college provides numerous opportunities for students to get involved in research, internships, and experiential learning, enabling them to gain practical experience in their field of study. Whether you are interested in pursuing a career in business, technology, or the arts, our undergraduate programs offer the foundation you need to succeed in today's dynamic world.

B.Com
Bachelor of Commerce

Our Bachelor of Commerce program at our college provides students with a comprehensive education in business, equipping them with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in a variety of industries. The program covers a wide range of business topics, including finance, accounting, marketing, and management, providing students with a well-rounded education in the field. Our faculty comprises experienced business professionals who are dedicated to providing students with a rigorous and relevant curriculum that prepares them for a successful career in the industry. Moreover, our program provides students with numerous opportunities to gain practical experience through internships, co-op programs, and case studies, allowing them to apply what they have learned in the classroom to real-world situations. Graduates of our Bachelor of Commerce program are highly sought after by employers, thanks to the practical skills and knowledge they have gained during their studies. Whether you want to start your own business or pursue a career in finance, marketing, or management, our Bachelor of Commerce program will provide you with the foundation you need to succeed in the business world.

BBA-Aviation
Bachelor of Business Administration - Aviation Management

The core curriculum in our college is specifically tailored to provide students with the essential knowledge and practical experience they need to succeed in their chosen major or program. The core curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the subject matter and includes courses that are specifically required for each major. These courses may include introductory classes, in-depth study of the major subject, and practical training that prepares students for careers in their field. The core curriculum ensures that students develop a strong foundation of knowledge and skills that are essential for success in their chosen profession. Through the core curriculum, students gain the expertise and confidence necessary to excel in their major and make a positive impact in their future careers.

BCA
Bachelor of Computer Applications

The elective curriculum in our college allows students to explore their personal interests and develop additional skills and knowledge outside of their major or program. With a wide range of elective courses to choose from, students have the opportunity to tailor their education to their unique goals and aspirations. The elective curriculum is designed to provide flexibility and allow students to pursue a variety of interests, from creative arts to advanced technology. Students can take courses that deepen their knowledge of their chosen field or broaden their horizons by exploring new subjects. This curriculum provides students with the opportunity to develop additional skills that can give them a competitive edge in the job market, such as leadership, teamwork, and critical thinking. The elective curriculum enhances the overall educational experience, enabling students to build a well-rounded set of skills and knowledge that will serve them well in any future endeavor.
