Principles of Management

This subject covers the fundamentals of management and leadership principles, including planning, organizing, staffing, directing, and controlling.

Marketing Management

This subject focuses on the principles and practices of marketing, including market research, advertising, sales, and branding.

Financial Accounting

Students learn about the basic principles of accounting, financial statements, and financial analysis, and how to use accounting software.

Business Communication

This subject teaches students how to communicate effectively in a business setting, including writing emails, reports, and presentations, and public speaking.

Human Resource Management

This subject covers the management of human resources, including recruitment, selection, training, performance appraisal, and compensation.

Business Law

This subject covers the legal aspects of business, including contracts, intellectual property, employment law, and international trade law.

Financial Management

Students learn about financial decision-making, including capital budgeting, risk management, and financial analysis.

Organizational Behavior

This subject covers the study of individual and group behavior in organizations, including motivation, leadership, communication, and organizational culture.

Operations Management

This subject teaches students about the management of business operations, including production, quality control, supply chain management, and inventory management.

Entrepreneurship

Students learn about the principles and practices of entrepreneurship, including idea generation, business planning, and small business management.

International Business

This subject covers the principles of global business, including international trade, cross-cultural management, and foreign investment.

Management Information Systems

This subject covers the use of information technology in business, including database management, information systems development, and business intelligence.
