Women's club

Our college offers an exceptional learning experience for young women, and its Women's Club is just one example of the supportive and empowering community that the college offers. The Women's Club at our college provides a safe and inclusive space for female students to come together, share ideas, and support each other in their personal and professional growth. Through the Women's Club, students have access to a wide range of activities and resources designed specifically for women, including mentorship programs, networking events, and career development workshops. The club also promotes gender equality and encourages women to take on leadership roles in their academic and professional lives. With the support of the Women's Club at our college, female students can develop the skills and confidence they need to succeed in any field they choose, and become the future leaders of tomorrow.

Dhanwantari Academy for Management Studies, is a learning centre for undergraduate and post graduate programs.
