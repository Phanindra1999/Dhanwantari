Chartered Accountancy India

Our Chartered Accountancy India program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of accounting and finance, along with the practical skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the highly competitive field of chartered accountancy.

Our program is taught by experienced professionals who are experts in their fields and who are committed to providing students with a rigorous and challenging education. Our program includes a mix of classroom instruction, practical training, and real-world experience, which allows students to develop the skills and confidence needed to succeed in the accounting profession.

Our program also provides students with opportunities for networking, mentorship, and career development, which can help them build their professional networks and launch successful careers in the accounting and finance fields. At our college, we are dedicated to providing our students with the tools, resources, and support they need to achieve their goals and become successful professionals in their chosen fields.

Global Financial Markets

Our Global Financial Markets program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the financial markets and the global economy. Our program is taught by experienced professionals who are experts in their fields and who are committed to providing students with a rigorous and challenging education.

Our program includes a mix of classroom instruction, practical training, and real-world experience, which allows students to develop the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the highly competitive field of global finance. Our program covers topics such as investment banking, financial risk management, international trade and finance, and financial market regulation.

Students also have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience through internships and other experiential learning opportunities, which can help them build their professional networks and launch successful careers in the global financial markets. At our college, we are dedicated to providing our students with the tools, resources, and support they need to achieve their goals and become successful professionals in the rapidly evolving field of global finance.

Certified Financial Planner - USA

Our Certified Financial Planner - USA program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the financial planning process and the skills and knowledge needed to become a certified financial planner. Our program is taught by experienced professionals who are experts in their fields and who are committed to providing students with a rigorous and challenging education.

Our program covers topics such as retirement planning, investment planning, tax planning, risk management, and estate planning. Students also have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience through internships and other experiential learning opportunities, which can help them build their professional networks and launch successful careers as certified financial planners.

Our program is designed to prepare students to pass the Certified Financial Planner exam, which is a globally recognized credential for financial planning professionals. At our college, we are dedicated to providing our students with the tools, resources, and support they need to achieve their goals and become successful professionals in the financial planning industry.
